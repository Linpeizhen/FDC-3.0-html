What is FineDayClub

FineDayClub was established by KKday Group in December 2020. It is a brand-new top-level customized life experience platform to realize the luxury and beauty of your dreams. Go up the mountain, go to the sea, the end of the world, the extreme of the earth, the Michelin star, and the experience of aged wine. Fragrance... FineDayClub is tailor-made for you, and through the honorable service of one-on-one consultation by a dedicated person, you can easily start a journey with both depth and breadth.
